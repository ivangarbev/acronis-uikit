Add `@acronis-platform/icons-sprite`: optimized SVG sprites (combined,
monocolor-only, and multicolor-only) generated from
`@acronis-platform/icons-svg`. Monocolor symbols are themable via
`currentColor`; symbol ids follow `m-<name>--<size>` / `c-<name>--<size>`. The
generated `sprites/*.svg` artifacts are committed.
